Moves
=====
Pokemon has moves collection.

Types
-----
Move -> Self
Move -> Attack
Move -> Environment

*Self* Includes healing and buff moves.
*Attack* Includes damage dealing moves.
*Environment* Includes moves that change the battle environment.
